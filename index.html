What some of These Features do!

    Hyperlapse: Wooaahhh that’s fast Jam pack all the action into a speedy hyperlapse with TimeShift mode. Up to 6x faster than real life.
    Timelapse: Make time fly by Pick a spot and record a timelapse from any angle. At night, turn on Nightlapse mode and GO 2 will use its brains (and AI) to adjust the exposure and intervals. It’ll blow your socks off
    Slow Motion: Oooooh slow-mo Couldn’t handle the speed? Slow a key moment down to 4x slow-mo in super-smooth 120fps.
    HDR Video: All the colors Pump up the colors with HDR mode. Get shots in a tap that look like they were edited by a pro. Ideal for stationary shots
    Auto Editing: The AI is taking over... your edit with FlashCut 2.0. AI takes your favorite clips, puts 'em in a story and edits them together on beat to music. Choose from themed templates or let AI freestyle. Don’t worry, it’s not coming for your job… yet.
    AquaVision: Make water crystal clear. Ugly blue haze in your underwater footage? Turn on AquaVision in the app. Upgraded from the bottom up, the algorithm now produces even more realistic and vivid colors than before.
    IPX8 Waterproof: Dunk it to 13ft. Surfing, pool party and skinny dipping ready. A bit like your spring break… only it’s an action cam
    Fits in your feed: Choose from 16:9, 9:16 or 1:1 aspect ratios to make sure your shot looks good anywhere you post it.
